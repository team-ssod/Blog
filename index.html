About Us

Team SSoD is a community which was formed from different parts of cities in Tamilnadu making the world safer place right from children to the elders by showing our excellence in Security research.

SSoD works on the basis of mentoring and guiding the youngsters by seniors and experienced members. This empowers the people to learn the stuffs in faster and avoid the mistakes done by the seniors.

Community was founded on December 2020 which is one of the leading CTF team in INDIA with 24 active members. The Squad is running by the students and professionals themselves by helping each other.

We as a team participate in CTFs (Capture The Flag) consistently and have ranked fifth team in INDIA according to CTFtime:) We routinely post our writeups in our blog.

The team members fascinately involve in practicing, learning and gain knowledge by solving HTB, tryhackme, pentesting labs and share their walkthroughs and experience in it.
